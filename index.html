This application turns your computer keyboard into the piano keyboard!

You can play chords with your left hand & melody with your right and/or you can play both hands on the whole QWERTY row and some digits (see keybord visualization below).

To play chords press some of below keys on your keyboard (left hand):

A for a-moll

C for C-dur

D for d-moll

X for E (E key is used already for melody...)

F for F-dur

G for G-dur

To play melody press shown below keys on your (physical) keyboard.

(these keys have the same position as on a piano keyboard - look at the interactive keyboard below)

Red buttons highlight C sound on a keyboard.
