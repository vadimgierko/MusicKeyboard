MusicKeyboard App

This application turns your computer keyboard into piano keyboard!

You can play chords with your left hand & melody with your right!

To play chords you need to press some of below keys with your left hand:

A for a-moll

C for C-dur

D for d-moll

E for E7

F for F-dur

G for G-dur

To play melody you need to press some of below keys with your right hand:

(these keys have the same position as on a piano keyboard - look at the interactive keyboard below)

6 for Gis

Y for A

7 for Ais

U for B

I for C

9 for Cis

O for D

0 for Dis

P for E

[ for F

= for Fis

] for G
